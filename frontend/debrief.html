Study Debrief

This study examined how different interface design patterns influence user decision-making, trust, and satisfaction.

Some participants interacted with an interface that included design elements commonly referred to as "dark patterns". These are interface techniques that may influence users toward certain choices.

The version you experienced was part of a controlled experiment and did not involve any real financial transactions, subscriptions, or actual charges.

The data collected will be used only for academic research purposes.

Thank you for your participation.
